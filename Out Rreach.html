Reaching out to university students from needy families.

A cry of the University students, some coming from really needy families along the way they fail to continue with their carrier cause of finances.
So far we have managed to support like four of them and two have completed their course as Medical Doctors by the Grace of God, But the demand is really high. As a church we are looking forward to start up a Sponsorship program in the Name of the Lord and we know it will happen soon.

Wheel chair distribution.

For those with disabilities and handicapped we reach out to them with wheel chairs and walking sticks so far once and we are looking forward to do it again by the Grace of God.

Offering Medical services
to the Underprivileged 

We being in a local community many families cannot afford standard Health because its costly and so as a church so far every year we have managed to reach out to the community with a medical camp,
With good Medical professionals in the various fields : Ophthalmologists, Cardiologist, Dental,Doctors, and many others.
