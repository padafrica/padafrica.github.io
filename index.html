What We Do! 

Empowering African marginalized rural women social economically through access to affordable health services and environmental conservation.

Maternal & Child Health

Promotion of safe motherhood and child health by Providing information on fistula,immunisation and link-up patients to case and integrating patients into the community. Learn more.

Reproductive & Adolescent Health.

PADAFRICA focuses on Prevent child marriages and sexual abuse of girls and adolescent through increasing access to information in schools, creating information centres and raising girls voice.

Hepatitis Immunisation & awareness in the community

Currently, PADAFRICA is implementing a Hepatitis B Community Awareness Project in partnership with NOPLHB in districts of Kasese. Learn more.

HIV & Hepatitis Sensitisation

We focus on Capacity building to the women, young mothers and children with disabilities, living with HIV/AIDS.
Learn more.

Women Empowerment

Training of rural women on Sustainable agriculture, maize growing and livestock rearing with domestic violence and HIV/AIDS, Viral Hepatitis awareness as cross cutting issues.

Environmental protection and Nutritional education campaigns

Community capacity building on climate change absorption strategies, tree planting, conservation of nature . Learn more.

Community Mobilisation towards development.

Involving community stakeholders in all programs of the organisation as partners at all levels. Learn more.

Keeping Girls in School

Provision of educational support and scholastic materials to children especially girls & those with disabilities in schools and Provision of educational guidance and counselling. Learn more.

Zero Torrence to discrimination

We focus n Proper planning and implementation of the organizational activities and programs without discriminating on grounds of colour, sex, religion, ethnicity or race. Learn more.

Our Ultimate Role in the Community

  • Mobilisation and Awareness
  • Project Implementation
  • Institutional Development and Sustainability

This helps create awareness about our specific activities, increasing access to and utilization of our services at the community, District and National level.

The organization implements the project to the target community in accordance with implementation plan engaging the community leadership, government and the donor partners

This aims to build an effective and well funded organization that empowers its staff and members (beneficiaries) to advocate and play a role in the implementation of the organization projects
