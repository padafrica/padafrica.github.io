Maternal & Child Health

Promotion of safe motherhood and child health by Providing information on fistula,immunisation and link-up patients to case and integrating patients into the community. Learn more.

Reproductive & Adolescent Health.

PADAFRICA focuses on Prevent child marriages and sexual abuse of girls and adolescent through increasing access to information in schools, creating information centres and raising girls voice.
Learn more.

Hepatitis Immunisation & awareness in the community

Currently, PADAFRICA is implementing a Hepatitis B Community Awareness Project in partnership with NOPLHB in districts of Kasese. Learn more.

HIV Sensitisation

We focus on Capacity building to the women, young mothers and children with disabilities, living with HIV/AIDS.
Learn more.

Women Empowerment

Training of rural women on Sustainable agriculture, maize growing and livestock rearing with domestic violence and HIV/AIDS, Viral Hepatitis awareness as cross cutting issues.
Learn more.

Environmental protection and Nutritional education campaigns

Community capacity building on climate change absorption strategies, tree planting, conservation of nature . Learn more.
