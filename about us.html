Our Background

PADAFRICA is a registered NGO for women and girls for sustainable improved welbeing, household income and driven development.
PADFRICA was founded in 2013 and registered at the District level with Community Development Services - Kasese under registration number,CBO/1749

PADAFRICA catalyse action by bring together adverse voices and interests that drive progress for gender equity with particular focus on maternal, sexual and reproductive health, improve Water, Sanitation and Hygiene in communities, schools to increase girls access to education , economic empowerment and environmental conservation
The organisation has became a stand out NGO demonstrating a community based approach in the transformation and development of communities.

PADA is now a well established CBO that show case our community at its best giving a holistic approach in health, sanitation, education agriculture and environmental conservation

Since its inception PADA have carried out many welfare and developmental issue based programs especially for the empowerment of Women, Children, Youth, the elderly, People living with HIV/AIDS, viral hepatitis and those with disabilities. They work to provide support and counteraction programs with a vision of translating skills into meaningful expression of Social Action and Development.

Rationale

PADA was formed mainly to reach out to the poor, needy and vulnerable persons in the society being referred to as the underprivileged.

PADA started with an aim of working with these type of people – the underprivileged, so as to work with them modalities that will sustain them for development and uplifting hence making them self sustaining.

Focus segment.

PADA focuses onto providing affordable health services, economical empowerment of women and girls and weakened and environmental protection, education is the main key issues of focus by the organization.

Our Specific Objectives.

In order to achieve its mission and realize its vision in the long run, PADAFRICA will pursue the following strategic objectives:

1

To advocate for quality reproductive and maternal health care services for women, girl and newborns

2

To promote water, sanitation and hygiene in the community and schools to increase girls access to education

3

To generate knowledge and provide advisory services and information on environmental protection and conservation

4

To empower women and girls through capacity building training , provide skills and cross learning visits.
