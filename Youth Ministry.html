WHO WE ARE

ICC Youth is more than just another youth service. ICC Youth is a culture of University students being built towards their callings, doing life together, and playing a direct role in seeing their lives and country changed. We gather on Wednesday nights at 7p at church from there we go out together, pushing one another to be all that God has designed us to be. ICC Youth is more than something you attend, it's a community that becomes a part of you.
