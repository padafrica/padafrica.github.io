Our Mission

Love GOD, Love Others, Serve the World

website builder

Love God, Love One Another, and Advance God’s Kingdom through lives transformed by God’s Love, Power, and Grace

We want everyone to know who Jesus truly is – not just his name, but that Jesus is God the Son, that he conquered sin and death on the cross, that His words are true, and that God desires to be in relationship with every person in the world.

Our passion and purpose to see people come to know Christ extends to the ends of the earth. We’re committed to eradicating Bible poverty, supporting people in crisis, and resourcing the local Church.

As a church we want to be living examples of what it’s like to live trusting God and knowing our identity as His sons and daughters. We’re intentional in focusing everything we do to help people live from God’s presence, empowered by the Holy Spirit.

Our Beliefs

  •  We believe the Bible to be the inspired, the only infallible, authoritative Word of God. God is the author of the Bible but was written with inspiration of the Holy men led by the Holy Ghost.
  • 2 Timothy 3:16 " All scripture is given by inspiration of God and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness that the man of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work
  • We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, in His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, in His bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and in His personal return in power and glory.
  • We believe that for the salvation of lost and sinful people is through faith in Christ alone. Regeneration by the Holy Spirit is absolutely essential.
  •  We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a Godly life.
  • We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; they that are saved unto the resurrection of life and they that are lost unto the resurrection of damnation.
